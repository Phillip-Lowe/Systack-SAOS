About Knight Train

Knight Train — the Midnight Night Train — is an independent wrestler based in Arkansas. With a powerhouse style and old-school grit, Knight Train brings a new force to the ring.

"All aboard. The Knight Train is coming through — and nobody's getting off at their stop."

Managed by Chris Randle, the Knight Train persona combines the unstoppable momentum of a runaway locomotive with the precision of a seasoned brawler. Whether it's a local Arkansas promotion or a regional showcase, Knight Train delivers every time.
