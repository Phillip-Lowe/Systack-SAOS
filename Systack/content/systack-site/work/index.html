Utopia Deli

Local deli in Little Rock, AR • Online ordering system with payment processing

Online Ordering Payment Processing Kitchen Notifications Square Integration

❌ The Problem

  • No online ordering — customers had to call
  • Phone orders led to errors and long hold times
  • Kitchen staff manually wrote down every order
  • No payment upfront — customers sometimes didn't show
  • Busy lunch rush meant missed calls = lost revenue

✅ What We Built

  • Custom online order form — branded to the deli
  • Full menu with modifiers and options
  • Square payment integration — pay when ordering
  • Kitchen notification — orders appear in the queue with all other orders
  • Order confirmation sent to customer
  • All orders logged in Google Sheets
