The Hidden Cost of Phone Tag

Every missed call is a customer who found someone else.

❌ BEFORE — Manual Booking

  • Customer calls. You're busy. No answer.
  • Customer texts. You reply 2 hours later.
  • "Are you free Tuesday?" Back-and-forth begins.
  • "How much for XYZ?" You explain again.
  • They want to book but need to "check their schedule"
  • They never call back. You lost them.

✅ AFTER — SyStack System

  • Customer clicks your link
  • Sees your services, prices, availability
  • Selects service + adds options
  • Pays deposit via Square
  • Gets confirmation instantly
  • Shows up at scheduled time. Done.
