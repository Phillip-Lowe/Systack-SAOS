Frequently Asked Questions

Is this AI consulting?

No. Systack does not sell "AI strategy" or "AI readiness assessments." We sell named, deployed automations that process invoices, extract data, classify documents, and respond to customers. You get working systems, not slide decks.

What's the difference between this and ChatGPT/Claude?

ChatGPT and Claude are cloud-hosted general-purpose chatbots. Your data goes to their servers. Systack Private runs entirely on your hardware with zero external data transmission. Systack Accelerate runs on private cloud instances behind encrypted tunnels. Neither shares your data with foundation model companies. And neither is a chatbot — they're purpose-built automation pipelines.

Do I need technical staff to run this?

No. Systack manages everything. For Private tier, we install the hardware, configure the network, and maintain the models remotely. For Accelerate, there's nothing on-site. You interact with reviewed outputs — emails, extracted data, classified documents — not with models or servers.

Can I switch between tiers?

Yes. Start with Accelerate, prove ROI, then move to Private when privacy requirements or volume justifies it. Setup fees are credited toward upgrade if done within 6 months.

What automations are NOT included?

We don't automate client-facing chat without human review. We don't automate legal advice, medical diagnosis, or financial recommendations. We don't build public-facing AI chatbots. We don't train custom models from scratch.

How long until I see results?

Private tier: 2–3 weeks from contract to live automations. Accelerate tier: 1 week. Most clients report significant time savings within the first month.
