## Slide 4 — The Architecture: How It Actually Works

**Title:** Your Data. Your Rules. Our Infrastructure.  
**Subtitle:** The technical details that make "private AI" actually private.

### Key Talking Points

**Systack Private Architecture**
- Dedicated Mac Studio or Linux server + NVIDIA RTX 4090 installed in your office
- Models: llama3-70b, command-r — enterprise-grade reasoning
- Fully air-gapped option available — zero outbound AI traffic
- Management via secure Tailscale tunnel (outbound mgmt only; AI data stays local)
- HIPAA, PCI, attorney-client privilege compatible

**Systack Accelerate Architecture**
- Secure cloud GPU instances (RunPod/Lambda Labs) in US data centers
- WireGuard VPN tunnel (Tailscale) — encrypted point-to-point
- Models: llama3-8b, mistral-7b — sub-2-second inference
- Auto-scaling: volume spikes handled automatically
- SOC 2 compliant infrastructure

### Suggested Visual
- Architecture diagrams: two simplified network maps
- Private: office → server → firewall → "NO OUTBOUND AI TRAFFIC" label
- Accelerate: office → encrypted VPN tunnel → cloud GPU → data flow arrows
- Clean, technical but not intimidating. Blue and gold color coding.
