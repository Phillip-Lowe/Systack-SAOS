## F. The Loom Video Script (3 Minutes)

**Hook (0-15s):**
"Hey [Name], saw your food truck at [location]. Built something that might save you time and get you more orders."

**Demo (15-90s):**
- Screen record phone
- Show text conversation: "Can I get a #3 combo?"
- Show agent responding: "Got it! Anything else?"
- Show order confirmation email
- "That's it. Customer texts, you get an email."

**Value (90-150s):**
- "No app to build. No commission to pay."
- "Customers already know how to text."
- "Takes 15 minutes to set up. I handle everything."

**Close (150-180s):**
- "$299 a month. Cancel anytime."
- "Want to try it? Here's the link: [Stripe payment link]"
- "Questions? Text me back."
