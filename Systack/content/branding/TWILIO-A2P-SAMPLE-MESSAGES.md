### Sample 5: STOP Confirmation (Auto-Reply)
**Campaign:** System — opt-out confirmation

```
You've been unsubscribed from Utopia Deli texts. To resubscribe, 
reply START. For help, reply HELP or call (501) 551-5944.
```

---

## Variables Used (in brackets)
- `[customer name]` — pulled from contacts database
- `[event date]` — pulled from catering form submission

## Content Types
1. **Promotional** — weekly meal prep menu, specials, catering
2. **Reminder** — deadlines, pickup notifications  
3. **Transactional** — confirmations, opt-outs

## Opt-Out
All promotional messages include: "Reply STOP to opt out."
All emails include unsubscribe link in footer.
