## Available Packages

### 1. Automated Invoice Ingestion System

**Target:** Law firms, medical practices, accounting firms, finance teams  
**Pain:** Manual invoice data entry, 15-30 min per invoice, high error rates  
**Solution:** Local AI pipeline that reads invoices → extracts structured data → pushes to accounting software

**Files:**
- `invoice-ingestion-mvp.md` — Full service package with pricing, architecture, ROI calculator
- `sales-one-pager.md` — One-page brief for outbound/email

**Pricing Tiers:**
| Tier | Setup | Monthly | Best For |
|------|-------|---------|----------|
| Premium (Local) | $8,500 | $1,200 | 500+ invoices/mo, strict privacy |
| Standard (Cloud) | $3,500 | $650 | 100-500 invoices/mo, cost-conscious |

**ROI Example:** 300 invoices/mo = $67,500 annual savings, 497% ROI, 2-month payback
