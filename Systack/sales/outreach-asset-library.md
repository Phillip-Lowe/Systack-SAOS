## 4. Discovery Call Script

### Pre-Call Setup
- Review prospect's website/LinkedIn
- Check for recent news, expansions, or pain signals
- Confirm calendar invite sent with video link
- Have demo environment ready (clean, relevant data)

---

### Opening (2 minutes)

"Hey [First Name], thanks for taking the time. Before we dive in, I want to make sure this is valuable for you—so I'll ask some questions about how you're handling [invoices/documents/support] today, where the friction is, and what "better" looks like. Then I'll show you exactly how SAOS handles that, and we can both decide if it makes sense to keep talking. Sound good?"

*[Pause for confirmation]*

"Quick context: SAOS is an AI operations system. We automate invoice processing, lead qualification, customer support drafting, document classification, scheduled reports, and data entry. Business tier is $299/month, Enterprise is $799/month. Most clients are saving 10+ hours a week in the first month."

---

### Discovery Questions (15 minutes)

**1. Current State — Invoices/Documents**
"Walk me through what happens when an invoice comes in today. Who touches it, what do they do, and how long does it take?"

**2. Volume & Scale**
"How many invoices, documents, or support tickets are you processing per week? Has that changed in the last year?"

**3. Pain Points**
"Where does the process break down or slow down? What's the most frustrating part for you or your team?"

**4. Current Tools**
"What are you using today for this—QuickBooks, Excel, a DMS, something else? How's that working for you?"

**5. Staff Impact**
"Who on your team is spending the most time on this? What would they be doing if they weren't?"

**6. Errors & Rework**
"How often do mistakes happen—wrong coding, missed invoices, duplicate payments? What does that cost you?"

**7. Visibility & Reporting**
"How quickly can you tell me how much you owe, what's overdue, or what your approval pipeline looks like?"

**8. Growth Plans**
"If you 2x or 3x your volume in the next year, can your current process handle it without adding headcount?"

**9. Previous Solutions**
"Have you tried to automate or outsource this before? What happened?"

**10. Success Definition**
"If we talk again in 90 days and you're thrilled with the decision, what would have changed?"

---

### Demo Positioning (5 minutes)

"Based on what you told me, here's what I'd focus on if I were you:"

*[Tailor to their answers—pick 2-3 features max]*

**If invoice pain:**
"SAOS reads invoices via email or upload, extracts line items, matches to POs, codes to your GL, and routes for approval. Your team reviews, not keys."

**If document pain:**
"Documents get classified by type, routed to the right workflow, and data gets extracted into your system of record. No manual sorting."

**If support pain:**
"Incoming support requests get drafted responses based on your knowledge base. Your team edits and sends, not writes from scratch."

*[Show, don't tell—screen share if on video]*

"This is the Business tier at $299/month. Enterprise at $799 adds multi-location, custom integrations, and advanced reporting. Most of our [restaurant/accounting/logistics] clients start on Business and upgrade when they expand."

---

### Close / Next Steps (5 minutes)

"So, [First Name], based on what we've covered—does this feel like it would move the needle for [Company Name]?"

*[Listen]*

**If yes:**
"Great. Next step is a 30-minute deep-dive where we connect SAOS to one of your actual workflows. I'll need [specific data/format]. Does [day] at [time] work?"

**If maybe:**
"Totally fair. What's the hesitation—timing, budget, or something about the solution itself?"

*[Address objection, then re-close]*

**If no:**
"No problem at all. Can I ask what would need to be true for this to be a yes? If it's not us, I want to at least point you in the right direction."

*[If still no—exit gracefully, send resources, set 6-month follow-up]*

## 5. Objection Handling Library

### 1. "Too Expensive"

"I hear that a lot. The real question is: what's it costing you *not* to have this? If you or your team are spending 10+ hours a week on paperwork at $25–50/hour, that's $1,000–$2,000/month in labor cost—plus errors, delays, and opportunity cost. Most clients break even in the first month. Want to run the numbers for your specific situation?"

---

### 2. "Already Using Something Like This"

"That's great—it means you already see the value. What I usually find is that teams have a partial solution: maybe QuickBooks for accounting, or a basic DMS, but the actual reading, routing, and reconciliation still happens manually. SAOS connects to what you have and automates the gaps. What are you using today, and what's still falling through the cracks?"

---

### 3. "Need to Think About It"

"Absolutely—this is a decision worth thinking through. To help, can I ask what's the specific hesitation? Is it timing, budget, fit, or something else? Once I know that, I can either address it now or send you exactly what you need to make the call."

---

### 4. "Send Me Some Information"

"Happy to. To make sure it's actually useful and not generic, what are you most interested in—how the automation works, what the setup looks like, pricing, or case studies from [their industry]? I'll send exactly that."

---

### 5. "We Don't Have the Budget Right Now"

"Understood. Budget cycles are real. Two questions: when does your fiscal year reset, and is this the kind of thing that could be prioritized if the ROI case is strong? I'm happy to put together a business case you can use internally."

---

### 6. "I Need to Talk to My Partner"

"Makes total sense—this affects the business, not just you. What usually works best is if I join that conversation or send a short summary your partner can review. What's their biggest concern likely to be—cost, disruption, or results?"

---

### 7. "Does This Replace My Current Staff?"

"No—it upgrades them. SAOS handles the repetitive data entry and routing. Your team handles exceptions, relationships, and higher-value work. Most clients tell us their staff is happier because they're doing work that actually uses their brains. Have you had pushback from your team about the manual work?"

---

### 8. "Is My Data Safe?"

"Yes—this is a question we take seriously. SAOS is SOC 2 Type II compliant, uses AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit, and your data is never used to train models. We can also run on-premise or in your existing cloud environment if your compliance requirements demand it. Want me to send over our security whitepaper?"
