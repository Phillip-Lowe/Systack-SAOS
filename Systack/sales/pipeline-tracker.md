## Outreach Sequence Templates (from CHATTY Library)

### Email Sequence (10-day, 3-touch)
1. **Day 1:** Personalized intro + industry case study
2. **Day 4:** Follow-up with specific pain point + soft CTA
3. **Day 7:** Breakup email — "I'll stop reaching out after this"
4. **Day 10:** Move to cold/nurture list

### LinkedIn Sequence
1. **Day 1:** Connection request (personalized note)
2. **Day 3:** Accept → send value message (no pitch)
3. **Day 7:** Share relevant content/comment on their post
4. **Day 14:** Direct message with case study

### SMS (after email engagement)
1. **Post-reply:** "Great hearing back! Got 15 min this week for a quick call?"
