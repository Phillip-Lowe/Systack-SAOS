## Description: <br>
Lightweight LinkedIn prospecting and outreach workflow for researching qualified leads, applying simple prioritization, drafting concise personalized messages, exporting clean CSV or Google Sheets-ready lead lists, and summarizing campaign activity. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Sales, growth, and business development users use this skill to define ICP-based LinkedIn targeting, qualify visible or user-provided prospect data, draft concise review-ready outreach, and prepare CSV or Google Sheets lead workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Prospect data can include personal or business contact information that may be subject to law, platform policy, or internal retention requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Use the workflow only for lawful, policy-compliant prospecting; minimize fields, keep CSV or Sheets files in controlled locations, and set retention limits. <br>
Risk: Generated outreach can contain unsupported personalization or claims if source evidence is weak. <br>
Mitigation: Manually review messages before sending, use only visible or user-provided evidence, and keep weakly supported messages role-based rather than specific. <br>
Risk: Automated or deceptive LinkedIn activity can violate platform safeguards. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill for research, qualification, drafting, exports, and reporting; do not use it for hidden sending loops, deceptive automation, or bypassing safeguards. <br>
