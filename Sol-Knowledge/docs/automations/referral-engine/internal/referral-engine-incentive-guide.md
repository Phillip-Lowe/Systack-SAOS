## 1. Program Models

### Model A: Flat Credit (Simplest)

| Party | Reward | When |
|-------|--------|------|
| Referrer | $10 account credit | After referee completes first booking |
| Referee | 10% off first visit | Applied at checkout |

**Best for:** Getting started quickly, testing referral behavior.

---

### Model B: Tiered Rewards (Gamified)

| Referrals/Month | Referrer Reward |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| 1–2 | $10 credit each |
| 3–5 | $15 credit each |
| 6+ | $20 credit each + "Top Referrer" badge |

**Best for:** Encouraging power referrers, creating social proof.

---

### Model C: Service-Based Rewards

| Party | Reward |
|-------|--------|
| Referrer | Free add-on with next service (e.g., free beard trim) |
| Referee | Free add-on with first service |

**Best for:** Service businesses where credits feel less tangible than free upgrades.

---

### Model D: Double-Sided Discount

| Party | Reward |
|-------|--------|
| Referrer | 20% off next visit |
| Referee | 20% off first visit |

**Best for:** High-margin services where discounts drive volume.

---

## 2. Reward Rules (Recommended)

| Rule | Value | Rationale |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Credit expiration | 90 days | Encourages timely rebooking |
| Max monthly rewards | 5 per referrer | Prevents abuse |
| Min referee spend | Full-price service | Prevents self-referral |
| Unique referee | Must be new customer | Prevents repeat abuse |

---

## 3. Anti-Abuse Measures

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Self-referral (same person) | Check email/phone uniqueness |
| Fake bookings for credits | Require completed service, not just booking |
| Credit farming | Monthly cap (5 referrals) |
| Expired credit complaints | Clear expiration in notification |

---

## 4. Program Launch Checklist

- [ ] Referral links generating correctly
- [ ] Attribution tracking working end-to-end
- [ ] Rewards applying automatically
- [ ] Notifications sending
- [ ] Anti-abuse rules configured
- [ ] Dashboard tracking metrics
- [ ] Terms communicated to customers

---

## 5. Optimization

| Metric to Watch | If Low | If High |
|-----------------|--------|---------|
| Participation rate | Increase reward, improve promotion | Consider tiered program |
| Conversion rate | Simplify referee experience | Increase reward for referrer |
| Cost per acquisition | Reduce reward | Program is efficient — scale it |
