## The Complete Customer Loop

```
1. DISCOVER          Customer visits link
     ↓
2. BOOK              Books + pays (Order System ✅)
     ↓
3. REMIND            Gets reminders (No-Show Prevention 🚧)
     ↓
4. SHOWS UP          Confirms or slot auto-releases
     ↓
5. UPSELL            Gets adaptive suggestions (Upsell Intelligence 📋)
     ↓
6. REVIEW            Leaves review (Review System 📋)
     ↓
7. REBOOK            Gets "due for next service" (Smart Rebooking 📋)
     ↓
8. REFERS            Shares link, gets reward (Referral Engine 📋)
     ↓
9. REPEAT            → Back to step 2

---
MONITOR: Owner sees Revenue Dashboard (📋)
RECOVER: Abandoned bookings chased (Missed-Lead Recovery 📋)
RETAIN: Memberships auto-renew (Subscription Engine 📋)
OPTIMIZE: Gaps auto-filled (Scheduling Optimizer 📋)
```
