# Systack Sales Call Script

## Opening (15 seconds)
> "Hi, this is Phillip with Systack. I help [restaurants/service businesses] in [city] turn their website into a customer magnet — online ordering, booking, and lead capture. Can I ask what you're currently doing for online orders right now?"

## Discovery (2-3 minutes)
- "Are you taking orders by phone only?"
- "Do you have a website that lets people order or book online?"
- "How do you handle leads that come from Google or social media?"
- "What's your biggest frustration with getting new customers online?"

## Pivot to Value
> "Most [restaurants/service businesses] I talk to lose 20-30% of potential orders because customers can't order or book online. We fix that in under 48 hours — a professional site with your logo, your menu/services, and a direct line for customers to place orders or book appointments."

## Pricing (When They Ask)
> "We start at $149 a month, which includes the site, hosting, and lead capture. Most clients see their first online order within a week. There's also a small setup fee to get everything customized to your brand."

## Objection: "I already have a Facebook page"
> "Facebook is great for social, but 60% of local searches happen on Google — not Facebook. If someone searches 'best [food/service] near me,' you want your own site showing up, not buried in a social feed. Plus, we capture the customer's info so you can reach them again. Facebook doesn't let you do that."

## Objection: "I can't afford it right now"
> "I get it — cash flow is real. Here's what we do: we can start with just the landing page and online ordering for $149. No setup fee this month if you sign up before [end of month]. You get your first online order, you see the value, then we talk about growing."

## Objection: "I need to think about it"
> "Totally fair. Can I send you a link to a demo site so you can see exactly what yours would look like? I built one for a [similar business] — [utopia-deli.systack.net if food, or systack.net if service]. Takes 2 minutes to look at."

## Close
> "What I'm hearing is [repeat their pain point]. Let's get you set up this week. I'll send over a simple agreement, we get your logo and menu, and you're live by [day]. Sound good?"

## Follow-Up (Text/Email if no close)
> "Hey [name], quick follow-up from our call. Here's the demo site I mentioned: [link]. If you want to move forward, just reply and I'll get you prioritized this week."

---

## Lead Scraper → Call Flow

1. Scraper runs at 6 AM daily → finds 40 new leads
2. You check list in morning
3. Pick top 5 (highest relevance score or closest location)
4. Call using script above
5. Log result: `interested`, `callback`, `not interested`, `closed`
6. Move to next 5 tomorrow

**Target:** 5 calls/day × 20% close rate = 1 new client/week
**Revenue:** $149–$449/mo recurring + $500–$2,000 setup
